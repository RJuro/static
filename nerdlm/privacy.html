Privacy Policy for PDF Digester

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how PDF Digester ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and handles your information when you use our service available at https://nerdlm.rjuro.com ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently and securely.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy and our Terms of Service.

1. What Information We Collect and Why

We aim to collect only the information necessary to provide and improve the Service.

Information from Users Authenticating via Google:

  • When you choose to log in or create an account using Google Authentication, we receive basic profile information from Google, specifically your Name and Email Address.
  • Why: We use this information to identify you within the Service, associate your generated content with your account, manage access, and communicate necessary Service-related information (e.g., policy updates, critical notices).
  • We also collect and store Usage Data associated with your account, such as the number of documents or audio files generated, to manage service limits as outlined in our Terms of Service.

Information Processed When You Upload Files:

  • When you upload a PDF file for analysis, it is transferred securely via HTTPS to be processed.
  • Crucially, we do not permanently store your original uploaded PDF files on our servers. The files are processed transiently (temporarily held) to perform the requested analysis and summarization.
  • This processing involves sending the content to third-party AI services (currently Google Gemini) as detailed in our Terms of Service and Section 12 (Data Subprocessors) below. The handling of your data by these third parties is governed by their respective privacy policies.
  • Why: This processing is essential to provide the core functionality of the Service â€“ digesting and summarizing your PDF content.

Generated Content You Choose to Store:

  • The Service generates outputs like summaries, reviews, overview PDFs, and audio files ("Generated Content").
  • If you are logged in, this Generated Content is stored and associated with your account (Name and Email Address) within our database (hosted on Supabase).
  • Why: We store this Generated Content to allow you to access, review, and download it later via the "My Generations" tab.

2. What Information We Do Not Collect

  • Browser Information: We do not collect information typically gathered from website browsers, such as browser type, language preference, referring site, or detailed IP address logs for general tracking purposes. Basic server logs necessary for security and diagnostics may contain IP addresses but are not used for user behaviour analysis.
  • Tracking Across Websites: We do not track your activity across other websites.
  • Sensitive Personal Information (Unless Uploaded): We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (e.g., health data, financial details) unless you voluntarily include such information within the PDF documents you choose to upload for processing. Please be mindful of the content you upload.
  • Commercial Exploitation: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your Personal Information (Name, Email, Usage Data) or your Generated Content with third parties for their commercial purposes.

3. Our Use of Cookies and Tracking

  • Necessary Cookies: The Service relies on Google Authentication and is built using Streamlit. These platforms may use essential cookies required for security, session management (keeping you logged in), and basic functionality. These are necessary for the Service to operate.
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for cross-site tracking, behavioural advertising, or detailed analytics of your browsing habits on our site beyond basic operational needs.

4. How We Secure Your Information

  • Technical Measures: We utilize Supabase for our backend infrastructure, hosted in Germany (EU). Supabase implements robust security measures. Data transmission (uploads, authentication) uses secure HTTPS protocols.
  • Access Control: Access to user data within our system is restricted to authorized personnel for operational and support purposes only.
  • Disclaimer: We take all reasonable measures within our control to protect your information (Account Information and stored Generated Content) from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. For highly sensitive information contained within PDFs, consider the risks inherent in uploading to any online service.

5. Global Privacy Practices and Data Location

  • Primary Storage: Your Account Information (Name, Email, Usage Data) and your stored Generated Content are stored and processed on Supabase servers located within the European Union (Germany).
  • Third-Party Processing: As mentioned, processing of uploaded PDF content involves third-party AI services (like Google Gemini) and TTS services (hosted on RunPod). These services may operate globally. We select providers committed to data privacy, but their specific processing locations are governed by their policies. Please refer to Section 12 (Data Subprocessors).

6. Resolving Concerns and Contacting Us

If you have any questions or concerns about how PDF Digester handles your personal information or this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

  • Contact Method: You can reach us via our dedicated email address: support@nerdlm.rjuro.com.
  • Response Time: We are committed to addressing your inquiries promptly and will respond within 30 days at the latest.

7. How We Respond to Compelled Disclosure

We may disclose Personal Information or Generated Content if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency) in accordance with Danish law. We will attempt to notify you about legal demands for your information when appropriate, unless prohibited by law or court order, or in emergency circumstances.

8. How You Can Access and Control Your Information

  • Account Information (Name, Email): As we use Google Authentication, your primary profile information (Name, Email) is managed through your Google Account settings.
  • Generated Content: If you are a registered user, you can access your stored Generated Content via the "My Generations" tab within the Service. You have the ability to delete specific generated items directly from this interface at any time.
  • Account Deletion: If you wish to delete your PDF Digester account entirely (which includes removing your associated Name, Email, Usage Data, and all stored Generated Content from our active systems), please contact us using the method described in Section 6. We will process your request subject to the data retention policies outlined below.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Original Uploaded PDFs: As stated, we do not retain your original uploaded PDF files after processing.
  • Generated Content: We retain your stored Generated Content associated with your account as long as your account is active, or until you choose to delete specific items via the "My Generations" tab. Furthermore, as outlined in our Terms of Service, we reserve the right to delete stored Generated Content associated with accounts that have been inactive for a continuous period of six (6) months.
  • Account Information: We retain your Account Information (Name, Email, Usage Data) for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will delete this information from our active databases. However, some residual data may be retained in backups or logs for a limited period as necessary for legal compliance, security, or operational purposes before being permanently destroyed.

10. How We Communicate With You

We will use the email address associated with your account (provided via Google Authentication) primarily for essential Service-related communications. This may include:

  • Responding to your support requests or inquiries.
  • Notifying you about important changes to our Service, Terms of Service, or this Privacy Policy.
  • Sending critical security notices.
  • Potentially notifying you about nearing usage limits, if applicable.

We will not send you marketing emails unless you provide separate, explicit opt-in consent for such communications. You will always have the option to unsubscribe from any marketing emails.

11. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Website and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. For material changes, we aim to provide notice (e.g., via email or a notice within the Service) at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Your continued use of the Service after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

12. Data Subprocessors

To provide the Service, we rely on certain third-party vendors ("Subprocessors") who may process your data on our behalf. Our key Subprocessors include:

  • Google: For user authentication (Google Identity Platform / OAuth).
  • Google: For AI-powered content analysis and processing (Google Cloud AI Platform / Gemini Models).
  • Supabase: For backend database hosting, storage of Generated Content and Account Information (Hosted in Germany, EU).
  • RunPod: For hosting the infrastructure used by our text-to-speech (TTS) service (Kokoro model).

We require our Subprocessors to adhere to strict data protection standards consistent with this policy and applicable laws like GDPR.
